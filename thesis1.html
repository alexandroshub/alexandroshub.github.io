01. THESIS_ABSTRACT

This paper investigates the emergence of complex global behaviors from simple local interactions within decentralized systems. By mimicking biological swarms (ants, bees, birds), we develop robust algorithms for collective robotics, optimizing pathfinding and resource allocation in environments where central control is impossible.
